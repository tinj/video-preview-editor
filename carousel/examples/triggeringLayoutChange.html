2 x 2 (GridLayout)

A key feature of the Famo.us Carousel is the ability to shift from 'focused' layouts to 'browsing' modes, allowing the user to easily sift through a lot of content with very little overhead for the developer.

Click on the Grid Icon to convert the carousel to a GridLayout, making the 60 items of content much more consumable.

Clicking on an item in the GridLayout will revert to a focused view.

Alternatively, click on the Coverflow icon to see another way to browse the content.
